The projects which I have developed.

01

Driver Drowsiness detection

During my final undergraduate year, I worked on a project aimed at reducing road accidents in India, where rising vehicle numbers and inadequate driver training are key issues. The system utilizes real-time data from cameras and MEMS devices to monitor driver conditions, issuing alerts through vibrations and notifications if drowsiness or distraction is detected. In critical situations, it can disable the ignition and activate hazard lights. OpenCV is the library used for image processing tasks in this project. The approach applies both Unsupervised Learning to uncover data patterns and Supervised Learning to predict outcomes based on labeled examples. This methodology, encompassing data collection, analysis, and algorithm development, seeks to enhance road safety effectively.

02

Omnifood Web Project

Introducing Omnifood, an innovative web-based platform dedicated to fostering health-conscious lifestyles through personalized meal delivery services. Utilizing cutting-edge AI algorithms, Omnifood curates daily meal options tailored to individual user preferences and dietary requirements. Crafted with HTML markup language and Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), the platform boasts a visually captivating interface, enriched by the strategic implementation of CSS Flexbox and Grid techniques. Subscribers benefit from the convenience of regular, nutritious meal deliveries, empowering them to maintain optimal health and wellness with ease and consistency.

03

SmartInventory DBMS Project

The SmartInventoryDBMS is a sophisticated database management system designed to streamline inventory control for small to medium-sized businesses. It utilizes a relational database model with SQL for real-time tracking of inventory levels, order management, and supplier coordination. The system efficiently updates stock information, manages purchase and sales orders, and maintains supplier records. It also provides detailed reports on inventory status and supplier performance while sending automated alerts for low stock and pending orders. Built with SQL and enhanced by OpenCV for data processing, SmartInventoryDBMS ensures precise and effective inventory management.
